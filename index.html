Inspiration

It doesn’t come to you,
You have to reach out to it.
You have to show commitment towards it.
And that commitment comes through movement.
Because it is movement that ensures…

in the right place
at the right time.

That helps
you find
your move.

That enalbles you to
leave a mark
on the universe

That lets you
groove to
your tune

And that makes
your dreams
come true.

Because in the end
it is movement that inspires us all.
